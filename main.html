Boys and Girls Club

We have started a new volunteering program at Boys and Girls Club. The mission of Boys and Girls Club is to inspire and enable all young people, especially those who need us most, to reach their full potential as productive, caring, and responsible citizens. As volunteers, we will be teaching young people of two age groups, 6-12 years old and 12-18 yeard old. However, they have different time sessions. For the age group from 6-12 years old, the time is 3:30pm-4:30pm. For the age group from 12-18 years old, the time is 6:00pm-7:00pm.

If you are interested, before you go, you have to attend an orientation once and once only and submit a volunteering application form. If you have not submit the application form, please print them out, fill them in, and bring it to the orientation.

We will be going to Boys and Girls club together as a group. The dates have not yet been decided. We will update on the schedule.
